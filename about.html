SK
Seema Kakade Ahuja

Plan Happy Wealth was born from a pattern seen across 25+ years in the financial industry: capable, hard-working people whose wealth never truly compounded — not because they earned too little, but because no one had helped them structure it.

Too often, families were sold products instead of being given a plan. Decisions were made in reaction to markets, headlines, and pressure rather than around real life goals. The result was stress, hesitation, and wealth that drifted instead of grew.

Plan Happy Wealth exists to change that — to make financial decision-making simple, structured, and human. Because when your money has a clear structure, confidence follows naturally.
